        []{#FAIR}

        -   #### FAIR

                public static final ResourceAllocationFairness FAIR

            ::: block
            Make acquisitions happen in order. This may mean a
            high-permit count acquisition can block smaller ones queued
            behind it that could otherwise grab the semaphore.
            :::

        []{#FAST}

        -   #### FAST

                public static final ResourceAllocationFairness FAST

            ::: block
            Move lower-permit count acquisitions that can acquire the
            lock ahead of high-count ones that are blocked due to size.
            :::
    :::
